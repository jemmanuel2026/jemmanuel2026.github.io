Ahri

The Nine-Tailed Fox

Ahri made her solo debut as a pop star in 2013, and she stole the stage as the most gifted K-pop artist at the Pop Shine Awards. She subsequently took a break from the industry after recording five singles to rebrand herself as the main vocalist of K/DA.

Evelynn

Agony's Embrace

Evelynn is K/DA'S certified diva, a demon that thrives on the anguish of those on the planet of Runeterra. She and Ahri are the pop group's lead vocalists, having known each other long before K/DA was created.

Akali

The Rogue Assassin

She was born into a martial arts dojo but left to pursue her dream of becoming an artist. She can still use a kama, and she channels her expertise into a hip-hop ninja figure. She perfected her act on the streets before becoming famous, and she likes returning to her origins whenever possible, appearing with other street performers in the towns she visits.

Kai'Sa

Daughter of the Void

Kai'Sa, who won the Can You Dance competition in Hong Kong in 2018, spends hours in the studio producing choreography for K/DA, thinking that each movement should portray fragility and power at the same time.
